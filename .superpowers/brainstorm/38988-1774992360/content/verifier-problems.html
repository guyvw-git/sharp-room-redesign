🔴 Rubric — scores the wrong things for room redesign

CURRENT (wrong)
  • Reference image match — compares to a golden that doesn't exist for new designs
  • Geometry completeness — checks for holes, not whether the RIGHT geometry is present
  • Color fidelity — checks color accuracy, not whether materials look realistic
  • Splat density — checks if sparse, not if new content has appropriate density
  • Edge quality — floater check only, no semantic content check
NEEDED (new)
  • Room structure integrity — walls/floor/ceiling look like original, no damage
  • Content removal cleanliness — no bleed-through of removed furniture
  • Semantic correctness — does it actually look like what was asked? ("kitchen" → cabinets, counter, appliances)
  • New content realism — does the new content look 3D and plausible, not like flat colored boxes?
  • Integration coherence — scale, lighting, placement feel right for the room

🟡 Missing: multi-angle verification
