What makes this look good (not boxes)

Real IKEA dimensions

SEKTION base = 70cm H × 38cm D. Units are exact, not guessed. Cabinet doors are modeled as frame + inset panel, 4cm border.

Material appearance

White laminate: smooth with warm off-white. Oak: sinusoidal grain along grain axis. Quartz countertop: veined pattern via FBM noise. AO darkening in corners.

Gaussian density

Current: ~80K/element. Target: 500K/m² of surface. Smaller splat scale (exp−5.5 vs exp−4.8) = sharper edges.

Room-aware placement

Everything placed relative to measured wall positions. Window clearances respected. "Won't put a cabinet in front of the window."
